Who is Rubber Duck

Welcome to Rubber Duck — a team of four passionate developers united by our love for padel and technology. We live where code meets the court, driven by a shared mission to simplify and elevate the padel coaching experience through smart, intuitive design.

Our goal is to make technology feel invisible — tools that just work, so players and coaches can focus on what truly matters: the game. From seamless scheduling to personalized training experiences, we're building a platform that removes friction and empowers everyone to play, learn, and improve effortlessly.

At the heart of Rubber Duck is a commitment to automation, innovation, and teamwork. Each of us brings a unique perspective, blending creativity with technical expertise to craft solutions that make life simpler — both on and off the court.

We're not just developing a platform; we're reimagining the future of padel coaching. Join us as we turn ideas into reality, fuelled by passion, precision, and maybe just a few too many cups of coffee.
